Sunflower Solar Tracking System

Revolutionizing solar energy efficiency through intelligent tracking

Explore Project

Project Overview

The robotics competition organized by Tech Talent Academy brought together seven schools in an innovative challenge. Our team developed a groundbreaking solar tracking system that maximizes energy efficiency.

Key Features

Dual-axis Tracking

Maximizes solar energy capture through precise sun tracking with 360-degree rotation and 180-degree tilt capabilities. Achieves up to 40% more energy collection compared to fixed systems.

Smart Sensors

High-precision photoresistors with real-time light intensity monitoring. Features automatic calibration and weather-adaptive sensitivity adjustments.

Energy Efficient

Ultra-low power consumption with sleep mode during low-light conditions. Includes power management system with only 0.1W standby power usage.
